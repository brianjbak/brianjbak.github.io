About Me

As the world continues to rapidly evolve, leveraging data has become crucial for allowing organizations to maximize their efficiency and productivity by identifying patterns, discovering insights, and promote strategic thinking. These developments inspired me to begin my career at Chapman University, having recently graduated in 2023 with degrees in Data Analytics and Business Administration.

In addition to the skills and concepts I learned at Chapman, I have had the fortune to work with NASCAR as a Data Strategy intern, engaging within the field of consumer behavior by learning about ticketing, crm, email, surveying, merchandise, and more.

These lessons and experiences have greatly influenced what I look for in my career, and I'm hoping to pursue a role that reflects these sentiments.
